Friday Wins Sessions a.k.a. “Show & Tell”

Friday Wins Sessions is our time to look back on the week and celebrate everything that has been accomplished. It’s when we pat each other in the back and re-energize for the week ahead.

Every team will share any demos, news, new closed deals, designs, closed projects, or any other milestone that they achieved during the week.

A good place to look for things to share at the Friday Wins Session are the Priority 1 (P1’s) tasks that were set and completed for that week related to your team’s OKR. But really, is about sharing any wins

For the time being, these sessions will be held at 10:30 AM CST every Friday. The sessions will be hosted in the following zoom link: https://zoom.us/j/790409781

If you have something you want to share during the session try to mention it to your manager before the meeting or give me a heads up. The sessions will be semi-“free form”; this means we’ll go into them with a couple of things in the agenda but will improvise and include/mention any wins on the stop. So feel free to speak up during the meeting if you have a win to share!

We’ll also use this opportunity to do Q&A’s regarding whatever subject you have in mind - we’ll do the questions until the end.
