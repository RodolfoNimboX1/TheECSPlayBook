Shared Spaces

On ECS offices we share a lot! Desks, chairs, tables, coffe spaces, restrooms, etc. So it is important to keep all of these clean, when we all are responsible for our coffee cups, chairs and desks, it all can be tidy.

Cleaning in the coffee bar:

The coffee bar is a very busy area, almost everyone wants a good cup of coffee to start their morning. Keeping this in mind every crumb of coffee and every drop of water (mixed with coffee, sugar or milk) can messy it up very quickly. When making your coffee in the morning try keeping it as tidy as possible, while making your coffee and after you finish it, bugs can appear very quickly when crumbs are left.

It is important to note that after you take your coffee you should always wash your coffee cup, everyone is responsible for what they use and the coffee cup is no exception.

Bathrooms:

The restrooms are the most important shared spaces!!! And the easiest to dirty!!! Everyone has needs and we all understand that (no shaming) but everything is important. Washing your hands, washing your teeth, anything and everything you do can easily make a mess and just a bit of spilled water can make a huge puddle.

Mostly, when washing your teeth remember that leaving toothbrushes in the restroom might just imply a contamination of the air in the restroom. It is much more dirty to keep it in the restroom than in your bag!

You desk / work space:

As you might know, most of us don’t really have desks so keep this in mind wherever you sit, the table is everyone’s responsibility, try not to leave used coffee cups, water glasses or plastic cups, crumbs, papers askew or anything else. Keeping a tidy space for everyone is not only good for cleanliness but for mental health, when your space is clean there are no outside distractions so you can focus fully on being productive.

Noise Pollution:

Keeping a cool and relaxed environment has always been a priority, keeping this is mind, it will never be condoned to have a good time and spend time and share good conversations with your coworkers. Nevertheless it’s always good to be mindful of the people that are quietly working close by, if you’re used to banging your feet, singing out loud or just making noise be mindful of others.
