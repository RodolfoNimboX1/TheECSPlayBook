FU’s: Friday Updates

What is a Friday Update?

A Friday Update is a weekly post where each team member shares an update on their OKR progress & confidence levels, last weeks & next weeks prioritized tasks, any blockers or risks, and any additional notes that might give more context to their post or just something extra they want to share.

It’s a standard format that everyone follows and allows us to keep a better track on our goals.

What exactly goes on the Friday Update?

Start with your OKR, and how much confidence per Key Result you have that you are going to complete it this quarter

Example

★ Pipeline Creation ★:

  • 100 Deals in Pipeline (Cirrus/Nebula) - (7/10 up 10%)
  • 25 Deals in Pipeline from ME (6/10 same)
  • $6M in Pipeline (5/10 down 10%)

It is helpful to show how your confidence level moved vs. last week to get a sense of the impact your prioritized tasks are having on your progress.

List last week’s prioritized tasks, and if they were achieved
If they were not, a few words to explain why. The goal here is to learn what keeps the organization from accomplishing what it needs to accomplish.

Example

Last weeks prioritized tasks (achieved yes/no, why?):

  • [P1] OKR mini-course [yes]
  • [P1] Push for 10 more candidates internship & start documenting projects [yes]
  • [P2] Experiment middleman + netlify cms [yes]
  • [P2] Rearrangement layout office monterrey [no] - will move to a P1 this week

Next list next week’s priorities:

  • P1 (Priority 1): Must do tasks
  • [P2 (Priority 2): Should do tasks

Only list three P1’s, and make them meaty accomplishments that encompass multiple steps. “Finalize spec for project xeno” is a good P1. It probably encompasses writing, reviews with multiple groups and sign off. “Talk to legal” is a bad P1. This priority takes about half hour, has no clear outcome, feels like a subtask and not only that, you didn’t even tell us what you were talking about! You can add a couple P2’s, but they should also be meaty, worthy of being next week’s P2’s. You want fewer, bigger items.

List any risks or blockers

Just as in an Agile stand-up, note anything you could use help on that you can’t solve yourself. As well, list anything you know of that could keep your from accomplishing what you set out to do— a business partner playing hard-to-schedule, or a tricky bit of technology that might take longer than planned to sort out.

Notes

You can use this last section to add anything else that doesn’t fit the format. It can be anything from a cool blog post you came across, to a link or attachment that shows something you worked on last week (like the result of one of your P1’s).

This section helps keep the Friday Updates more human & relatable. It also helps by adding context to everything else you’re sharing.

When and where do I post my Friday Update?

All Friday Updates are posted under the "Friday Updates" 1 category in Discourse.
Each week, whoever posts first from your team will open a new topic with the name format “Team Name Friday Update - Date”, e.g. “MGMT Team Friday Update - May 11th”.
All Friday Updates are posted between Friday afternoon and Monday morning. Most of the time the team’s weekly meeting (Monday/Tuesday) works as a hard dead line for everyone to post their FU.
